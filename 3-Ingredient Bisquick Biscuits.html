3-Ingredient Bisquick Biscuits

★★★★★

Bisquick, Bread

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 12 min Total Time: 30 min Difficulty: Easy Servings: 9 Source: (Swipe For Photos)

Ingredients

2 c Bisquick

1/2 c Sprite*

1/2 c sour cream or plain yogurt

1/4 c butter melted


*or 7Up or zero, any soda with the carbonation.

Description

The Sprite acts as a leavening agent, that makes your biscuit dough rise. The carbonation bubbles from the Sprite react with the baking soda in the Bisquick, and poof! You’ve got these fluffy, little bites of heaven! Very similar to older recipes with flour Mother's Cowboy Biscuits

Directions

Preheat oven to 450 degrees. Put the butter into a cast iron skillet or an 8×8 baking dish and place in oven while it is preheating so the butter melts. Watch and remove pan when butter is melted so it doesn't burn. It will take longer to heat to 450 than to melt the butter.

In a medium-sized bowl, stir together Bisquick, Sprite, and sour cream or yogurt until just combined.

Sprinkle the countertop with a little Bisquick. Add the dough to the counter and knead a little using your hands and pat the dough out to about 1/2" thick.

Add additional Bisquick as needed to form a very soft dough that is just holding together. Pat into a square and you can then use a knife and cut the square into square biscuits in a tic tac toe pattern or use a biscuit cutter or the top of a glass or just pat them out freehand, and place them in the pan of melted butter.

Bake for 12-15 minutes or until golden brown and cooked through.

Notes

Best enjoyed warm straight from the oven. However, you can also store them in an airtight container for up to 2 days.
