7-Minute Divinity Frosting

★★★★★

Icing/Frosting/Toppings/Sauces

Cook Time: 7 minutes

Ingredients

1 c sugar

1/4 tsp salt

1/2 tsp cream of tartar

2 egg whites

3 T water

1 T vanilla (or more)

Directions

Combine all of the ingredients except the vanilla in a stainless steel heavy-bottomed saucepan. Use a good stainless steel pot for this, definitely not one with a nonstick coating, because we are going to beat it for several minutes and all that mess would flake off and get in your icing – so don’t do that!

Place mixture over medium-low heat and beat with an electric hand mixer constantly for 5-7 minutes, or until icing is fluffy and stiff peaks form when the beaters are removed. If it doesn't fluff up significantly, then pour everything in a stand mixer bowl, set to medium speed, and it should come together fairly fast.

After removing from heat, don't forget to stir in the vanilla and incorporate it well!

Ice the cooled cake. This icing is very stable and keeps well for up to a week on an icebox cake stored in the refrigerator. Enjoy!

Notes

Note: You'll need two recipes of this icing to ice a layer cake. Fortunately, this recipe doubles beautifully, so there's no need to make two separate batches, just double it and make it all at once.
